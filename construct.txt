
Construction is the process of building or assembling structures, infrastructure, or facilities. It involves various activities, such as designing, planning, financing, and executing projects to create buildings, roads, bridges, dams, and other types of infrastructure. Here are some key aspects of construction:

Project Phases: Construction projects typically go through several phases, including planning, design, procurement, construction, operation, and maintenance. Each phase has its specific tasks and requirements.

Types of Construction: Construction can be broadly categorized into several types, including residential (houses, apartments), commercial (office buildings, shopping centers), industrial (factories, warehouses), and civil (roads, bridges, dams). Each type has unique considerations and regulations.
